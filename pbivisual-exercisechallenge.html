Exercise Challenge: Power BI Visual

The Story Behind the Visual

This visual was inspired by a ten-day exercise challenge where I exceeded my initial goals, showcasing the positive correlation between consistency and energy levels.

Visual Features

The visual includes:

  • A line chart tracking energy levels and heart rate over time.
  • A bar chart summarizing daily cumulative workout minutes.
  • KPIs for workout duration, max energy, and max mood.

Skills Utilized

  • Power BI
  • Data Visualization
  • Analytical Thinking
  • Storytelling with Data
