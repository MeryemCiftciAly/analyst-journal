Capstone Project

Merit America / Coursera (Google Data Analytics Program 2025)

Capstone Project Description: Healthcare Insights and Predictive Analytics

Anticipated Completion: January 2025 | Dataset from Data.gov [ TBA ]

Objective of the Capstone Project

The primary objective of this project is to analyze behavioral health data to uncover insights into the factors contributing to readmission rates among Medicaid recipients receiving mental health and substance use disorder services. Specifically, the project aims to:

  1. Investigate Key Metrics: Explore the relationships between follow-up care, medication adherence, and continuity of care with mental health and substance use disorder readmissions.
  1. Identify Risk Factors: Use predictive analytics to identify populations at greater risk of critical care readmissions, enabling targeted interventions.
  1. Drive Data-Informed Solutions: Provide actionable insights to improve patient outcomes and reduce readmission rates through enhanced post-discharge strategies.

Why This Project?

Healthcare data offers a meaningful context for developing analytical skills while addressing a topic that impacts lives. Additionally, it represents my journey into Python, expanding my skillset and preparing for more advanced analytics.
