About

How my journey began

Hi, I’m Kamara Rowe-Çiftçi—though to my family and friends, I’m simply Meryem.

I’m a data analyst with a genuine passion for uncovering insights that drive innovation and impact. My journey into the world of data began during my time in business administrative support, where I discovered my natural curiosity and love for numbers. I found joy in the details—organizing schedules, managing records, and, most notably, creating presentations that told a compelling story. I loved pulling reports from databases, structuring all the information meticulously, and ensuring every slide flowed seamlessly. I was always eager to get feedback, knowing that clear communication was key to making a real impact.

Eager to take this passion further, I transitioned into analytics by completing the Google Data Analytics Certificate Program through Merit America. This training not only deepened my technical skills—exploring trends, identifying patterns, and delivering actionable solutions—but also reinforced my belief that data, when used thoughtfully, can solve real-world challenges.

What Guides My Work?

  • Continuous Learning: Every challenge is an opportunity to refine my skills and grow both professionally and personally.
  • Collaboration: I thrive in environments where I can bridge technical insights with strategic goals, working together to achieve shared success.
  • Purpose-Driven Analytics: I’m driven by the idea that data has the power to create meaningful change, whether in business, health, or community initiatives.

Why I Love Working with Data

Even back in my administrative days, I was captivated by how data could tell a story. Every spreadsheet was a puzzle waiting to be solved, and I truly found joy in crafting presentations pulling reports, organizing data, and ensuring that each turn of the page communicated the right message. I was passionate about making sure that every detail was accurate and that the person receiving the information could easily understand it. This spark of curiosity seeing how simple numbers could transform into powerful insights has stayed with me ever since.

Imagine John Doe, who follows a morning routine that energizes him during the week but feels inexplicably drained by the weekend. By analyzing his sleep patterns, activities, and habits, we can uncover hidden factors and suggest improvements. This ability to look beneath the surface and solve real-life puzzles is what excites me most about data analytics.

As I continue to grow in this field, I look forward to learning more skills and tools and exploring different ways analytics can drive meaningful solutions whether in business strategy, operations, or beyond.
